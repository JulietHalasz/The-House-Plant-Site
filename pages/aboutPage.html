About Us

house plant enthusiasts

We’re a group of people with green thumbs and green minds. Beginning as a group task for the Introduction to Web Development Course by Code First Girls and Service Now, The House Plant Site quickly bloomed into a passion project. You won’t be-leaf the time we spent propagating this website, and we’re thrilled to finally be able to share it with you.

At its roots, The House Plant Site is designed to act as a space where fellow enthusiasts can learn more about their plants - and maybe even discover their next one!

Alana

Alana is a coder and UX designer. Her experience and interests vary widely: from website design, to illustration, animation, and even creating her own Instagram filter.

Alana has found keeping plants in the home a fun way to instantly brighten up a space, and can even make you feel more settled after a big move! Her favourite plant is a Bird of Paradise, because it's the most exotic and beautiful plant you can own.

Harriet

Harriet is a fashion marketer and entrepreneur, who helped launch and establish RELUV, an online charity shop website. Going forward, she wants to use her skills to help small businesses, while still finding time to look after her many house plants.

Her favourite plants are Swiss Cheese Plants because they are easy to look after, make a statement in the room and grow really big - and fast!

Juliet

Juliet is a writer and an illustrator. She loves cats, books, and her house plant Charlotte.

Charlotte is a Chinese Money Plant, and came as a gift from a friend’s cutting of their own plant. After a stressful train journey to get Charlotte home without damaging or jostling it, Juliet has been nervous (even more so than usual!) about making sure it’s well looked after, thanks to the sentimental value attached to the plant.
